Sakkhat ?
Sakkhat is providing a better platform for micro marketing. A meeting place for micro sellers and their buyers.
User Account
After creating an account user can access all posts and spaces and can filter them (Trending, Recent, Category), react on posts, pin a post, add a space to favorite. All these activities will be addressed to user account activity panel, where the user can get all the activity record.
Space
Space is the virtual shop where the sellers present their products with posts and manage them. Space admin have full control over the space to manage. Every individual account can create a single space which will be associated with the account. The owner account user can't give react and pinned own product posts. And also can't add the space to favorite list.
Product Post
A space can create a post for a product.
